🚀 EduSteam AI
The Ultimate AI-Powered Productivity Ecosystem for Students

EduSteam AI is a personalized learning assistant designed to bridge the gap between passive reading and active knowledge retention. By integrating custom LLM workflows, it transforms your study materials into interactive learning experiences.


✨ Key Features:

🧠 Active Recall Hub: Automatically generates smart flashcards and quizzes from your uploaded PDFs.
📄 AI Core Document Processor: Deep-dives into your documents to provide instant summaries and insights.
🎧 Focus Vibe Engine: Immersive ambient audio streams optimized for deep-work focus sessions.
⏱️ Integrated Study Clock: Built-in Pomodoro-style timer to maintain high-intensity study flows.


🛠️ Built With:

Dify AI: Orchestrating complex LLM workflows for document extraction.
Vanilla JS/HTML/CSS: Clean, lightweight architecture for high-performance UI.
YouTube Data API: Providing curated focus-based soundscapes.

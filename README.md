# EduSteam AI: The Ultimate AI-Powered Productivity Ecosystem for Students 💡

EduSteam AI is a personalized learning assistant designed to bridge the gap between passive reading and active knowledge retention. By integrating custom LLM workflows, it transforms your study materials into interactive learning experiences.

## ✨ Key Features

* 🧠 **Active Recall Hub:** Automatically generates smart quizzes from your uploaded PDFs.
* 📄 **AI Core Document Processor(Zephyr PDF Reader):** Deep-dives into your documents to provide instant summaries and insights.
* 🎧 **Focus Vibe Engine:** Immersive ambient audio streams optimized for deep-work focus sessions.
* ⏱️ **Integrated Study Clock:** Built-in Pomodoro-style timer to maintain high-intensity study flows.
* 🤖 **Brainy:** Your very own AI study mate! Have a queston or stuck in understanding a difficult question? Don't worry just ask it and you'll get your answer!

## 🛠️ Built With

* **Dify AI:** Orchestrating complex LLM workflows for document extraction and for Brainy's set up.
* **JavaScript/HTML/CSS:** Clean, lightweight architecture for high-performance UI.
* **YouTube links:** Providing curated focus-based soundscapes.
* **Lovable:** For building up the quiz generator
